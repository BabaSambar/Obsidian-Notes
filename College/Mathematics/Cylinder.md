**General equation**
$$
(x-\alpha)^2+(y-\beta)^2+(z-\gamma)^2=(\frac{l(x-\alpha)+m(y-\beta)+n(z-\gamma)}{\sqrt{ l^2+m^2+n^2 }})^2 + r^2
$$
****
Equation where axis is x axis:
$$
y^2+z^2=r^2
$$
Equation where axis is y axis:
$$
x^2+z^2=r^2
$$
Equation where axis is z axis:
$$
y^2+x^2=r^2
$$
